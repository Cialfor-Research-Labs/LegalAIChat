
LAW LLM User Manual`n1. Introduction`nLAW LLM is a web-based legal workspace that helps legal professionals intake facts, analyze legal issues, draft notices, and manage controlled access for users.`nThis manual is designed for:`nAdvocates and legal researchers`nInternal legal operations users`nAdmin users managing approvals and access`nLAW LLM supports legal work. It does not replace professional legal judgment.`n2. System Requirements`nModern browser: Chrome, Edge, or Firefox (latest recommended)`nStable internet connection for AI responses`nApproved LAW LLM account`nValid role-based access (User or Admin)`n3. Getting Started`n1. Open the LAW LLM web app URL provided by your organization.`n2. On the login screen, enter your approved email and password.`n3. If you do not have access yet, select **Request access**.`n4. After login, use the left navigation to open Legal Chat, Document tools, and Settings.`nWhat you should see:`nA dashboard/workspace with navigation and module cards`nTheme and session controls in the top area`n4. Account Access and Authentication`n4.1 Sign In`n1. Enter approved email.`n2. Enter password.`n3. Click **Sign in**.`nIf successful:`nYou are redirected into the workspace.`n4.2 Forgot Password`n1. Enter your email on the login page.`n2. Click **Forgot password?**`n3. Check email for a setup/reset link.`n4. Open link and set a new password.`nCommon mistakes to avoid:`nEntering a different email than the approved account`nUsing an expired link`n4.3 Request Access`n1. Click Request access on login.`n2. Fill first name, last name, email, organization, and use case.`n3. Submit request.`n4. Wait for admin approval.`n4.4 Set Password (First-Time Setup)`n1. Open one-time setup link from email.`n2. Enter new password and confirm it.`n3. Submit.`n4. Return to login and sign in.`n5. Navigation Overview`nThe main workspace typically includes:`nLegal Chat: Structured legal intake and analysis conversation`nDocument Generator: Draft legal notices/documents`nDocument Analyzer: Analyze uploaded legal text/documents`nSettings: Profile and password updates`nAdmin Access (admin role): User approvals, setup links, access history`nWhat you should see:`nActive module highlighted in the sidebar/navigation`nContext panel and content area for the selected module`n6. How to Use Legal Chat`nPurpose`nLegal Chat helps structure facts and guide legal issue framing before drafting or deeper analysis.`nSteps`n1. Open Legal Chat.`n2. Start with a short issue statement.`n3. Answer follow-up questions with concrete facts, dates, parties, and documents.`n4. Review suggested issues and AI analysis.`n5. Continue until the issue summary becomes clear and complete.`nInput quality tips`nInclude parties, timeline, jurisdiction, and key evidence.`nSeparate facts from assumptions.`nMention known relief sought (for example, payment, injunction, specific performance).`nWhat you should see:`nProgress/confidence indicators`nFollow-up question flow`nStructured outputs and issue framing`nCommon mistakes to avoid:`nVery short prompts without factual context`nMissing dates and jurisdiction`nTreating first output as final without verification`n7. How to Generate Legal Drafts`nPurpose`nGenerate structured legal drafts (for example, notices) from provided case facts.`nSteps`n1. Open Document Generator.`n2. Fill all required fields carefully.`n3. Add precise facts, chronology, and legal basis.`n4. Start generation.`n5. Review and edit the draft before external use.`nReview checklist before use`nNames, dates, addresses, and amounts are correct`nJurisdiction and legal references are applicable`nTone and prayer/relief align with case strategy`nFinal document is reviewed by a responsible legal professional`nWhat you should see:`nDraft sections rendered in a structured format`nStatus updates during generation`n8. How to Analyze Documents`nPurpose`nExtract insights from uploaded/provided legal content to support case preparation.`nSteps`n1. Open Document Analyzer.`n2. Upload or provide the document text.`n3. Trigger analysis.`n4. Review extracted points, risks, or summaries.`n5. Cross-check critical findings against source text.`nCommon mistakes to avoid:`nUploading low-quality or incomplete files`nRelying on extracted points without verification`n9. Admin Operations`nAdmin users can:`nApprove, deny, or manage access requests`nGenerate/manage password setup links`nMonitor access activity and user status`nTypical approval flow`n1. Open Admin Access Control.`n2. Review pending users and metadata.`n3. Approve or deny based on policy.`n4. If approved, ensure password setup process is completed.`nAdmin best practices`nApply least-privilege access`nReview logs periodically`nRevoke access promptly for inactive or unauthorized users`n10. Security and Privacy Best Practices`nDo not share credentials.`nUse strong, unique passwords.`nLog out from shared devices.`nEnter only necessary sensitive data.`nFollow organization data handling and retention policies.`nRestrict exports and sharing of generated outputs as per policy.`n11. Troubleshooting and Error Messages`nLogin failed / invalid credentials`nRecheck email/password`nConfirm caps lock is off`nUse forgot password if needed`nAccess pending or denied`nPending: wait for admin decision`nDenied: contact admin with use-case details`nForgot password link issues`nEnsure email matches approved account`nCheck spam/junk folder`nRequest a new link if expired`nSlow or incomplete AI response`nRetry with clearer, smaller factual chunks`nAvoid combining too many legal issues in one prompt`nRefresh session if response stalls`nDraft quality improvement tips`nProvide chronology and objective details`nSpecify jurisdiction and desired outcome`nInclude opposing party actions and supporting records`nWhen to contact admin:`nAccess, role, or account status problems`nWhen to contact technical support:`nRepeated system errors, failed loads, or generation failures`n12. Limitations and Responsible Use`nLAW LLM may produce incomplete or imperfect analysis.`nAI-generated legal text must be reviewed before filing or serving.`nVerify statutes, citations, deadlines, and jurisdiction applicability.`nFinal legal responsibility remains with the human legal professional.`n13. FAQ`nIs LAW LLM a replacement for a lawyer?`nNo. It is an assistive legal productivity tool.`nCan I use generated notices directly?`nOnly after legal review and necessary edits.`nWhy am I not able to log in?`nPossible reasons: wrong credentials, no approved access, or setup incomplete.`nCan admin see all users?`nAdmin can view and manage users relevant to the configured access system.`nWhat should I do if output seems inaccurate?`nRefine prompts, add facts, and verify results against sources and legal standards.`n14.
